# Presentation Demo Script

**Total Estimated Time**: ~5-7 Minutes

## 1. Introduction (1 min)
"Hello everyone! Today we are presenting the **Smart Study Planner**. We noticed that students struggle not just with *what* to study, but *how* to manage their time, stay motivated, and easily parse massive amounts of syllabus material. We built a solution to fix this."

## 2. The Core Problem & Setup (1 min)
*(Action: Log into the application and show the Dashboard)*
"When you log in, you're greeted by a calming, distraction-free dashboard. It gives you a bird's-eye view of your exam countdowns and your immediate tasks for the day."

*(Action: Click on 'Subjects' -> Show a Subject with Chapters)*
"We allow students to break their massive subjects down into manageable chapters. Once they input this data, our proprietary **Smart Timetable Engine** takes over..."

## 3. Smart Timetable & Focus (1.5 mins)
*(Action: Click 'Planner' and show the timetable)*
"...It automatically generates a schedule based on how close the exam is and the difficulty of the subject. But scheduling isn't enough; you actually have to do the work."

*(Action: Click 'Focus Space', start the timer)*
"So, we built an integrated Pomodoro timer. You can go full screen to block out distractions, toggle ambient study music, and just focus. When the timer finishes, it automatically logs your progress to the backend."

## 4. Gamification & Analytics (1 min)
*(Action: Click 'Achievements' and then 'Analytics')*
"To solve the motivation problem, we implemented gamification. Every logged hour earns you XP. You level up, earn badges, and build daily fire streaks. Over in the **Analytics** tab, you can view your 30-day activity heatmap, subject-proficiency radar charts, and export it all as a PDF."

## 5. The Crown Jewel: RAG AI (1.5 mins)
*(Action: Click 'Library' and then 'AI Assistant')*
"Finally, we integrated Google's Gemini LLM. But we went a step further than a standard chatbot. We implemented **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** using ChromaDB. A student can upload their specific class PDF. They can then switch the AI into 'Chat with Notes' mode, and the AI will scan *only* the uploaded PDF to answer questions, ensuring absolute accuracy without hallucinations."

## 6. Conclusion (30 seconds)
"In conclusion, we've built a full-stack, deeply integrated, AI-powered productivity ecosystem. Thank you, we're open to questions!"
